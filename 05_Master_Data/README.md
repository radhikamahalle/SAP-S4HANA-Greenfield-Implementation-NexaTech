# Master Data

## Objective

Master Data is the foundation of every SAP business process. It stores consistent business information that is reused across Procurement, Inventory Management, Sales, Production, and Finance.

This project focuses on creating and maintaining Master Data required before executing business transactions.

# Business Partner

## Transaction Code

BP

### Purpose

Business Partner is the central master record in SAP S/4HANA used to maintain Vendors, Customers, and Business Entities.

### Activity Performed

- Created Organization Business Partner
- Maintained General Information
- Maintained Address Details

# Material Master

Material Master stores all information related to a material used in purchasing, inventory management, production, and sales.
